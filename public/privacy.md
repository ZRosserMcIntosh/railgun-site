## Introduction

Rail Gun ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Rail Gun application ("App").

## Our Privacy-First Design

Rail Gun is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- **End-to-End Encryption**: All messages and calls are encrypted on your device before transmission. We cannot read your messages.
- **Minimal Data Collection**: We collect only the data necessary to provide the service.
- **Pseudonymous Billing**: Subscription payments use anonymized references, not user IDs.
- **No Third-Party Tracking**: We do not sell your data or use advertising trackers.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

| Data Type | Purpose | Encryption |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| Username | Account identification | Stored hashed |
| Display Name | Profile display | Stored encrypted |
| Email (optional) | Account recovery only | Stored encrypted |
| Password | Authentication | Stored as Argon2 hash |

### Information Generated by Use

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Device Keys | E2E encryption | Until you delete device |
| Message Metadata | Delivery routing | 30 days (no content) |
| Login Timestamps | Security monitoring | 90 days |

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Message content (encrypted, we cannot read)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contact lists
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Device identifiers beyond session
- ❌ Voice/video call content

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide the Service**: Route messages, connect calls, deliver notifications
2. **Security**: Detect abuse, prevent fraud, enforce terms
3. **Billing**: Process subscriptions (pseudonymized)
4. **Improvements**: Aggregate analytics (no individual tracking)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data only:

1. **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly agree
2. **Service Providers**: Hosting (encrypted data only), payment processors
3. **Legal Requirements**: Valid legal process (we challenge overbroad requests)
4. **Safety**: To prevent harm or illegal activity

### Law Enforcement

We will:
- Require valid legal process
- Challenge overly broad requests
- Notify users when legally permitted
- Provide only what we're legally required to

**Important**: Due to E2E encryption, we cannot provide message content even if required.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Delete your account and data
- **Port**: Export your data
- **Object**: Opt out of analytics

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@railgun.app

### Account Deletion

When you delete your account:
- Username is released after 30 days
- All stored data is permanently deleted
- Encrypted messages remain encrypted (we never had the keys)
